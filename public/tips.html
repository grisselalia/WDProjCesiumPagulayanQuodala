Tips & Tricks for Travelers

This page serves as a Question & Answer guide to help players understand the core mechanics of the game. Whether you're new or experienced, these FAQs provide helpful insights to improve your journey.

Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What should I focus on as a beginner?

Focus on completing Archon Quests, exploring the world, and leveling only a small group of characters to conserve resources.

❓ How do I get stronger quickly?

Improve your character levels, upgrade weapons, enhance talents, and build teams with good elemental synergy.

❓ Are artifacts important early in the game?

Artifacts become more important later. Early on, prioritize main stats and focus on character progression instead.

❓ How should I spend my Primogems?

Save Primogems for limited-time character banners instead of the standard banner to get better long-term value.
