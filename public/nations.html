Mondstadt

The City of Freedom, blessed by the Anemo Archon Barbatos. Gentle winds, rolling plains, and a carefree spirit define this land.

Liyue

The land of contracts and commerce, shaped by ancient traditions and the legacy of the Geo Archon, Rex Lapis.

Inazuma

The Electro nation of Eternity, ruled by the Raiden Shogun. Thunderstorms and sakura blossoms coexist in rigid harmony.

Sumeru

The City of Wisdom, governed by the Dendro Archon. Knowledge, dreams, and nature intertwine throughout its rainforests and deserts.

Fontaine

The Nation of Justice, where law meets spectacle. Under Furina, Fontaine thrives on drama and hydro-powered innovation.

Natlan

The land of war and passion, forged in flame. Strength, honor, and volcanic fury define the rule of the Pyro Archon.
